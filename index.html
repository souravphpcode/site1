About Us


The Association of Engineers, India is a landmark at Bidhannagar (Salt Lake City) Kolkata was established by a group of committed nationalist Indian engineers in the year of 1919, during the British Raj, to strength professional fraternity among Indian Engineers, and to foster sustained professional ethics among them. The Institute is now poised to make its programme five-fold based on Education, Training, Consultancy and Inter- Institutional collaboration. ... Our Association has been promoting various knowledge upgrading and skill developing courses and training programme for engineers and technically qualified persons from weaker section of society. The Association is also engaged in imparting job oriented training to personnel in various disciplines of engineering and trades. All these courses are approved by the AICTE and are affiliated to the West Bengal State Council of Technical and Vocational Education and Skill Development.
